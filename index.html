Hodl. Earn.

Hodl is a Reflect ecosystem on the Binance Smart Chain.
Hodl works by applying a 3% fee to each transaction and instantly splitting that fee among all holders of the token.
Hodl's smart contract allows you to earn extra tokens by simply holding, without any token inflation.

Token Distribution

The HODL token distribution starts with a presale, unlike any other RFI fork therefore no one can abuse HODL tokens at low liqudity, and is the first RFI fork to exist on the Binance Smart Chain.

0% of the tokens will be kept by the team or for advisors - ensuring an entirely fair distribution and market.

Retail Investor Friendly
